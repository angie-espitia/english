Complete the text with the correct form of be (affirmative or negative).

Look at the Useful language box. Then choose and or but for sentences a-e.

  • a. My friend is in 8th grade and / but my brother is in 8th grade too.
  • b. I am Colombian and / but my best friend is French.
  • c. Tracey is 15 years old and / but her sister is 15 years old too.
  • d. Sam is from The United States and / but he is American.
  • e. My name is Helen and / but her name is Sally.
Useful language
and / but
Use and to connect two
complementary ideas.
(addition)
Use but to connect two
contrasting ideas. (contrast)

In your notebook, complete the text with information about you. Then write some sentences about your best friend. Use and and but to join sentences.
