14. You are going to raise money for a special cause. in groups, decide:

  • a. What special cause or region you are raising money for.
  • b. How you are raising money.
  • c. What each person is doing.

    • Example:
    I am wearing a bird suit.
    Juanita is selling cakes.
    We are all singing.

    15. Now present your plan to the rest of the class.

    WRITE

    16. Listen to the presentations by the other groups in the class and make notes.

    17. Choose three of the presentations and write about them. Which did you think was the best? Why?
