    FOCUS ON LANGUAGE

    7. Read the Useful language box. Then complete the table in your notebook.

    Useful language
  • We use adjectives to describe nouns. They usually appear before the noun (a high mountain, a beautiful park), or after the verb be (The sea is colourful.)
  • To compare two things we add –er to the adjective (a mountain is higher than a house). Or we add the word more before the adjective (The Caribbean is more colourful.)
  • To compare three or more things we add –est to the adjective (the highest mountain in the word). Or we use the most before the adjective (the most dangerous volcano in the world.)
