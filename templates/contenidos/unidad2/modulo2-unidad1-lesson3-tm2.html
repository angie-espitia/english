    • 6. Read the sentences and choose the correct option A, B, or C.

  • 1. My cousin works in a hotel in the school holidays. He _______ beds.
    •     A. make    B. makes     C. goes
  • 2. My grandmother helps my mum at home. She usually _______ dinner for us.
    •     A. washes    B. cook     C. cooks
  • 3. My brother is a student. He _______ a lot of homework.
    •     A. have    B. has     C. possess
  • 4. My friend is a security guard. He _______ up very late as he works all night.
    •     A. gets    B. washes     C. get
  • 5. My mother is a film critic. She ________ DVDs for her job.
    •     A. watch    B. make     C. watches
