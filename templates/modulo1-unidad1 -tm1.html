Useful Vocabulary
Good Morning

We say "Good Morning"

from when we wake up until 12 pm.
Good Afternoon

We say "Good afternoon"

after 12 pm until about 6 pm.
Good Evening

We say "Good evening"

after 6 pm until we leave or go to bed.
Good Night

We say "Good Night"

when we leave a place late at night or go to bed.
